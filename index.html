About our Daycare

Daycare Hours:
Academic day is 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM.
Extended day is 6:30 AM - 6:30 PM

Visit us:
We are located in West Buechel. Click HERE to get directions.

Young Leaders Academy caters to the educational needs of children 6 weeks to 12 years of age. Our Program employs an integrated curriculum which delivers a multi-sensory approach. We strive to foster a safe and stimulating environment where the creative abilities of each child are nurtured.

We nurture a love of Allah ﷻ and Islam in our students through stories and nasheeds. Islamic values and morals are a high priority with us. They are introduced to Arabic letters and begin to memorize short chapters of the Holy Quran from a very early age.

The backbone of our program is our caring, qualified, and dedicated teachers who are well trained and professional. They strive to help children grow into unique and confident individuals. Our teachers have or are working towards their child development associate (CDA) certification, CPR, and first aid training.

Our classrooms are self-contained, safe, organized and inviting. They provide a variety of learning components including, art, library, manipulatives, pretend play, building, science, mathematics, writing, computer and sensory centers.

A typical day in our preschool includes:

  • Circle Time
  • Learning Centers Time
  • Quran, Arabic, Islamic Studies
  • Small/Large Group Activities
  • Outdoor/Indoor Play
  • Lunch/Snack
  • Nap
